# AI Security Posture Check

A structured 10-minute check that scores your organization's AI security across
six dimensions and tells you exactly where the gaps are before an exposure
happens.

Built by MBL Partners (https://mblpartners.com) — part of the Thrive with AI
skill library.

---

## What It Does

Runs you through six scored questions across the dimensions that decide whether
AI adoption becomes an advantage or a liability:

1. Data Classification — How well does your team understand what's sensitive?
2. Shadow AI / Tool Inventory — Can you name every AI tool in active use?
3. Usage Policy — Do you have rules governing how AI interacts with your data?
4. Access Controls — How are permissions and data access actually controlled?
5. Incident Response — If an exposure happened this week, who owns the response?
6. Governance Ownership — Who holds accountability for AI security specifically?

Plus one context question on regulatory exposure to tailor the output.

After all answers are collected, it scores your responses (6–24) and produces
a posture snapshot: what's covered, where you're exposed, and 1–3 specific
actions to close in the next 90 days.

---

## Score Tiers

| Score | Tier      | What It Means                                                                     |
|-------|-----------|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| 6–11  | Exposed   | Adopting AI faster than governing it. A data leak is a matter of when, not if.   |
| 12–17 | Reactive  | Some controls exist, but gaps are wide enough to put you in response mode.        |
| 18–21 | Managed   | A real posture is forming. Specific gaps remain — close them in parallel.         |
| 22–24 | Governed  | Building security in step with adoption. Focus on maintaining it as tools shift.  |

## Install

1. Download `ai-security-posture-check.skill`
2. Open Cowork
3. Drag the `.skill` file into your Cowork window
4. The skill installs automatically and is ready to run

---

## How to Run It

Once installed, trigger it by saying any of the following:
- "Check our AI security"
- "Are we exposed?"
- "AI security posture"
- "Data leak risk"
- "Is it safe to roll out AI?"

The skill asks one question at a time and waits for your response before moving
on. It takes about ten minutes.

---

## Output

At the end you receive a Posture Snapshot with:
- Your score and tier
- The 1–2 dimensions where you're most covered
- The 1–2 gaps most likely to cause an exposure
- Specific, 90-day actions sized to your actual situation
- A regulatory note if you operate in a jurisdiction with AI or data requirements
